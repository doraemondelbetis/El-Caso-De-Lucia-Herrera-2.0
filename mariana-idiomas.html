English — Grammar

Present Perfect vs Past Simple
• Past Simple → finished actions "He left on Thursday."
• Present Perfect → unfinished or unclear time "He has been busy these days."
Exercise:
"I ___ (see) him near the university last Saturday."
"He ___ (say) he was out of town for seven days."
